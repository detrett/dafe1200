What

Innovation Nation is an organization that arranges innovation camps revolving around frugality. We provide participants with a professional step by step experience in the world of frugal innovation.

Every year during the summer we organize intense 3-day long camps every two weeks. For detailed information visit the Schedule section of our website.

Why

We want to create an environment that pushes participants to learn about the benefits of frugal innovation. We believe in the benefits of our teachings and the experience we provide.
