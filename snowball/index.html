Zero hunger in the globe.
You can help now.
Find out how.

Step One

Gather your donations.

Ready to donate? Collect non perishable food that you want to give away. Good examples are rice, other grains and canned goods. Make sure they not expiring soon!

Step Two

Find your closest delivery point.

Find the nearest delivery point and bring your food with you. You will be assisted by a worker that will take your food and exchange it for a voucher that contains a redeemable code.

Step Three

Create an account.

In order to redeem or gift your voucher into points that can be spent at certain stores, you will need to create an account first. In your profile, you will also be able to keep track of accumulated and spent points.

Step Four

Once you've redeemed your voucher you can simply use your points or give them away too!
