Artificial Reefs

Artificial reefs generally provide a stable growing area for corals, and habitat for fishes and all the other organisms that you would find on a natural reef. Artificial reefs can be built out of several different materials such as concrete, cement, glass or steel rebar. The diversity of materials allows reefs to be built in all types of shapes or sizes. This creates room for design to come into play.

By highlighting design as a major element of artificial reef building, we offer our users the opportunity to leave their own personal mark while saving the ocean. Creating artistic reef structures helps migrate tourism, as more people visit these structures instead of natural reefs. Tourism can be a threat to coral reefs, as accidental or intentional damage occurs.

How to design your reef with ReefStarter

There are two ways to do it: You can import your own designs into the app or you can build them using the built-in designer.

Once the design has been submitted, it will be moderated to ensure that it is suitable as a future habitat. We recommend you to visit our sources to ensure success. This might be a bit frustrating initially, but you will be given suggestions to get it right, so do not give up!

To help with this, our designer tool has been created with a modular structure. This lets you get creative without any concerns by choosing from our preselected modules.

Whether you choose to submit your own design, you will be able to browse through all the submitted designs around the world through the app. The map allows you to filter designs by areas and to see which areas are most in need of help.

Once you have found your favorite design, you can submit a vote to let us know. Choose wisely, you only get one per area!

HOW IT WORKS

Once per area, you can submit one vote to their favorite design. By donating to our cause, you strengthen your vote and your account. The more you donate, the more your vote is worth.

You can change your vote anytime until the cycle of donations is over. Keep checking, you might find a new favorite design! Once enough donations have been collected to build a structure, a winner is selected.

All designers, voters, donators and of course winners, get badges and progress for participating in each cycle. Our goal is to encourage you to contribute -no matter how much- and have something to show off for it. All winners will get their own piece of creation built and set up to start helping reefs recover as well as a special recognition badge.

After a structure is installed, we also take pictures of it and add it to our gallery, so you can browser all the past contributions.

Learn more by checking our sources.
